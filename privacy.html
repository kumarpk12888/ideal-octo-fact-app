Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Merge PDF (also referred to as "PaperStack") is a simple utility app that merges PDF files together. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not require account creation and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Your Files

All PDF files you add to the app are processed entirely on your own device. Files are never uploaded to any server, and no file content ever leaves your device. When you close the app, any files you added are cleared from memory.

Permissions

The app may request access to your device's storage solely to let you choose PDF files to merge and to save the merged result. This access is used only for that purpose.

Third Parties

This app does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect any data to begin with.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this same page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer through the app's listing page on the app store where you downloaded it.
